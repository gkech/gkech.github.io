About

2022-10-14

I work at Beat as a Software Engineer, focusing on the Driver experience withing the app.

Before Beat, I was member of the Personalisation & Social Network Analysis Group at National Centre of Scientific Research “Demokritos” working on Dynamic Community Analysis topics. I received my Master in Engineering in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Technical University of Crete. There I worked on research projects on Multi-agent Systems applied on the domain of the Electric Vehicle charging (G2V&V2G).

I’m also working towards receiving a Master of Science in Computer Science from National and Kapodistrian University of Athens.
